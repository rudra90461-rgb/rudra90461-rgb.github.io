BharatJob Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 31, 2026

Welcome to BharatJob, India's Local Expert Network. Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application and website.

Disclaimer of Affiliation: BharatJob is an independent, private platform. It is NOT affiliated, associated, authorized, endorsed by, or in any way officially connected with the Government of India, any state governments, or any of their agencies or departments.

1. Information We Collect

To provide you with a seamless networking and marketplace experience, we collect the following types of information:

  • Authentication Data: When you log in, we collect your 10-digit mobile number to verify your identity via a One-Time Password (OTP).
  • Profile Information: We collect personal details you voluntarily provide, including your full name, profession/role, city/location, and WhatsApp number.
  • User-Generated Content: Any data you post on our platform—including expert bios, consultation fees, startup project descriptions, investment needs, job postings, and local market item details (e.g., food, handicrafts)—is stored on our servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the data we collect primarily to operate the BharatJob Super App:

  • To display your public profile and listings (Experts, Startups, Jobs, Market) to other users on the platform.
  • To facilitate direct communication between users by generating secure WhatsApp chat links based on the contact number you provide.
  • To maintain the safety, security, and integrity of our platform.

3. Third-Party Services

BharatJob utilizes third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:

  • Google Firebase: We use Firebase (Authentication and Firestore) to securely manage your login sessions and store app data. You can review Google's Privacy Policy here.
  • WhatsApp: Our app allows users to connect with you via WhatsApp. We do not have access to your private WhatsApp messages, but by providing your number, you consent to other users contacting you regarding your listings.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to others. The information you post in your public profile and listings is visible to other users of the BharatJob app to facilitate professional networking and local commerce.

5. Your Data Rights

You have full control over your data. You may update your profile details (Name, Role, City, Phone) at any time through the "My Account" section of the app. If you wish to delete a listing (e.g., a job or market item), you can do so directly from the app interface using the "Delete Post" button.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
